Introduction

We aim to raise awareness and share practical solutions for building a more sustainable and greener city. We are a group of young learners who are passionate about protecting the environment and improving the quality of life in our community. Our goal is to educate people about the importance of green living, including smart planting techniques, reducing pollution, and increasing green spaces in urban areas. Things You Should Know About GreenCity


Importance of GreenSpace

You already know GreenSpace is very Essential for our community, but HOW?

Explanation
  • Green spaces are essential in urban areas as they help improve air quality, reduce heat caused by buidlings and roads, and create a healthier environment for people. However, due to rapid urbanization and limited awareness, many communities lack sufficient greenery.
  • Through our digital platform, we aim to highlight the importance of green spaces by educating residents and sharing practical ideas to build a greener, more sustainable city.

Problems Caused By Lack Of GreenSpace

What challenges do a city face without enough green spaces?

Problems
  • Waste management issues: Recycling systems can be inefficient or underused.
  • Loss of biodiversity: The expansion of urban areas reduces natural habitats for plants and animals.
  • Poor Air Quality: A lack of trees and vegetation reduces natural air filtration, leading to higher levels of pollution.
  • Health Issues: Limited access to green spaces can increase stress and reduce opportunities for physical activity.

Solutions


Home Gardening

Home gardening is the practice of cultivating and nurturing plants—such as vegetables, fruits, herbs, or flowers—on a small plot of land or in containers immediately around a family residence.

RooftTop Gardening

Rooftop gardening means growing plants on the roof of a building. Instead of leaving the roof empty, people use that space to grow vegetables, flowers, in pots or garden beds.

Container Gardening

Container gardening is the practice of growing plants in containers such as pots, barrels, or boxes. It is a suitable solution for people with limited space, poor soil quality, or a need for a portable garden.
